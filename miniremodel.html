Mini Remodel

Consider the mini-remodel for a faster, more affordable kitchen transformation. Avoid the inconvenience, cost and stress of requiring permits and keep your current layout while achieving the look and feel of a fully remodeled kitchen. Here's what the mini-remodel includes:

  • Cabinets: We replace old doors, drawer fronts, hinges, and add custom-made ones. The cabinets will be covered with new material, including crown and base molding. We can also upgrade drawer boxes and add convenient accessories like trash pullouts and spice pullouts.
  • Counter-tops: Choose from custom fabricated quartz or natural stone counter-tops. Select your preferred style, edge detail, and layout
  • Back-splash: We install the back-splash of your choice, using your preferred material and layout. 

Refacing

If you’re happy with the current layout of your kitchen and wanted a quick upgrade, then refacing is a great option for you. With Keane Kitchens refacing system, we cover all fronts and exposed ends of your cabinets. We install new doors and drawer fronts along with new moldings and hardware. You can choose from painted, stained or laminate finishes. We also install new soft close drawer boxes along with soft close hinges on the new doors.

Benefits of refacing

  • Completely transform the appearance of your cabinets
  • Half the cost of full cabinet replacement
  • Full use of your kitchen in the evenings during the work
