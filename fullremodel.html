KITCHEN CABINETS

Keane Kitchens offers new custom frame-less cabinets along with installation. We build in-house and to your specifications. All our cabinets are built using the highest quality materials and finishes. We also install new soft close drawer boxes along with soft close hinges on the new doors.

Our process for new Cabinets

  • Make a appointment to visit our showroom
  • Bring your measurements and pictures along with the sizes for appliances you have or are planning to use
  • Make your choices from options that include:
  • A variety of wood species, stains, colors and finishes.
  • Door and drawer styles
  • Cabinet handles, hinges and drawer pulls
  • Decorative molding and trim work
  • We will provide a quote for your new cabinets
  • We can also do a 3D model for you to visualize your design

COUNTER-TOPS

If you’re happy with the current layout of your kitchen and wanted a quick upgrade, then refacing is a great option for you. With Keane Kitchens refacing system, we cover all fronts and exposed ends of your cabinets. We install new doors and drawer fronts along with new moldings and hardware. You can choose from painted, stained or laminate finishes. We also install new soft close drawer boxes along with soft close hinges on the new doors.

Benefits of refacing

  • Completely transform the appearance of your cabinets
  • Half the cost of full cabinet replacement
  • Full use of your kitchen in the evenings during the work
