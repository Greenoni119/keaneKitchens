PROFESSIONALS

Keane Kitchens is a family-owned business serving the Bay Area Peninsula and surrounding areas since 1992. Keane Kitchens is fully licensed, insured and bonded with a team who takes great pride in providing quality craftsmanship and excellent service. It is difficult if not impossible to tell the difference between cabinets refaced by Keane Kitchens and brand new cabinets. Our company expects nothing but the best for our clients which is quality, convenience, and value.

meet the owner

Patrick Keane has specialized in kitchen remodeling over the past 30 years as a licensed general contractor. Over those years, he has worked with countless homeowners who have gone through unnecessary stress, time and money to achieve the kitchen of their dreams. After seeing so many kitchens being torn apart and completely removed only to be replaced and put back in the same layout, he decided to build upon refacing instead of replacing cabinets and adding countertops and backsplashes to the service and created the Mini-Remodel with Major Results!!!!
